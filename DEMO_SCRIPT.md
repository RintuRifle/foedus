# Foedus — 2-Minute Demo Video Script

**Use:** LinkedIn/X launch post, resume link, YC-style demo.
**Tool:** Loom ya OBS. 1080p. Cursor smooth rakhna, koi dead air nahi.
**Prep:** Fresh account, ek achhi brochure PDF ready, DB me 10+ tenders seeded, worker running. Ek evaluation pehle se complete rakhna (backup, agar live wala slow ho).

---

### SHOT 1 — The Hook (0:00–0:15) · Landing page
*Screen: foedus landing, slow scroll nahi — static.*

> "Every year, Indian government publishes tenders worth lakhs of crores. But for a small business, finding the right one means reading 80-page PDFs at midnight — and one missed eligibility clause means instant rejection.
>
> This is Foedus. Six AI agents that do it for you."

### SHOT 2 — Magic Onboarding (0:15–0:40) · /onboarding
*Screen: brochure PDF drag-drop karo. Parse animation dikhne do. Review card reveal.*

> "No forms. I just drop my company's brochure… and watch.
>
> The AI reads it — sectors, turnover, certifications, past projects — and builds my company profile in twenty seconds. This profile is what my agents will use to hunt."

### SHOT 3 — Daily Matches (0:40–1:00) · /dashboard
*Screen: 2-3 swipes karo — ek left, ek right (stamp animations dikhein), fir ek card pe rukna.*

> "Every morning at 6 AM, Foedus scrapes government portals and matches new tenders against my profile. It's Tinder for tenders — reject, save…
>
> …and this one? 87% match. Solar plant, Bihar, right in my budget range. Let's evaluate."

### SHOT 4 — The Evaluation Theater (1:00–1:30) · Evaluate click → live progress
*Screen: "Evaluate with AI" click. Agent rail pe live progress. 10-15 sec dikhao (edit me speed-up ok), fir report reveal.*

> "Now watch — six agents, live. The Auditor is the star: it extracts every eligibility criterion from the tender PDF and cross-checks it against my actual documents.
>
> And here's the thing — every claim it makes is grounded. If the AI cites a clause, we verify that clause actually exists in the tender. No hallucinations. A wrong answer here costs a business lakhs."

### SHOT 5 — Report + Proposal (1:30–1:50) · Report → proposal
*Screen: compliance matrix scroll — green checks, ek red cross expand karo. Fir proposal open, ek second scroll, Export PDF click.*

> "The verdict: I meet six of eight criteria — and it tells me exactly what's missing: audited balance sheets. Fixable.
>
> And because I cleared the audit, the Writer agent has already drafted my technical proposal — reviewed by a second agent, exported as a submission-ready PDF."

### SHOT 6 — Close (1:50–2:00) · Landing ya pricing
*Screen: billing page ya landing.*

> "From tender discovery to a ready proposal — under two minutes, at ₹999 a month.
>
> Foedus. Tender dhundo mat — tender khud tumhare paas aaye."

## Recording notes
- **Pace:** script ~280 words = comfortable 2 min. Rushed mat karna.
- **Edit trick:** Shot 4 me evaluation ke 90s ko 10s me time-lapse karo (viewers ko "live" feel + speed dono milta hai).
- **Captions:** LinkedIn autoplay muted hota hai — burn-in captions zaroori.
- **Thumbnail:** compliance matrix wala frame (green/red visually strongest hai).
- **CTA comment:** launch post ke first comment me app link + "DM for early access".
