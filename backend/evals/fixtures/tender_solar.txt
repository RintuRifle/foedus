SECTION 4: ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA (PRE-QUALIFICATION)

4.1 The bidder must have an average annual turnover of at least Rs. 300 Lakhs
during the last three financial years (FY 2022-23, 2023-24, 2024-25), duly
certified by a Chartered Accountant.

4.2 The bidder should have satisfactorily completed at least one similar work
of grid-connected solar power plant of minimum capacity 1 MW, OR two works of
minimum capacity 500 kW each, during the last five years. Work completion
certificates from the client must be enclosed.

4.3 The bidder must possess valid GST registration in the State of Bihar or
undertake to obtain the same within 30 days of award of contract.

4.4 The bidder must be an MNRE empanelled vendor OR hold valid certification
under ALMM (Approved List of Models and Manufacturers) for solar modules.

4.5 The bidder must submit audited balance sheets and profit & loss statements
for the last three financial years.

4.6 The bidder shall have a valid Electrical Contractor License issued by the
Bihar Electrical Licensing Board or equivalent authority of any State
Government.

4.7 Joint Ventures / Consortiums are NOT permitted for this tender.

4.8 The bidder must not have been blacklisted by any Central/State Government
department as on the date of bid submission. A self-declaration affidavit on
Rs. 100 stamp paper must be submitted.

SECTION 5: SCOPE OF WORK

The scope includes site survey, structural assessment of rooftops, design and
engineering, supply of solar PV modules (minimum efficiency 20%), string
inverters, mounting structures, cabling, earthing, lightning protection,
net-metering arrangement with NBPDCL/SBPDCL, and commissioning. The contractor
shall provide comprehensive O&M for 5 years including module cleaning,
inverter maintenance, and generation monitoring with minimum CUF of 15%.

SECTION 6: FINANCIAL TERMS

Payment Terms: 70% on supply of materials at site, 20% on successful
commissioning, 10% released in equal annual installments over the O&M period.
Performance Bank Guarantee: 5% of contract value, valid for the entire O&M
period. Liquidated Damages: 0.5% of contract value per week of delay, capped
at 10%. Defect Liability Period: 5 years from date of commissioning.

SECTION 7: TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

Solar modules shall be Mono PERC / TOPCon with minimum 540 Wp rating, tier-1
manufacturer, with 25-year linear performance warranty. Inverters shall have
minimum efficiency of 98% with 5-year warranty extendable to 10 years.
All equipment must be BIS certified as applicable.
